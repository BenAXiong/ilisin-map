Option A trade-offs:
✓ Immediate geographic context. Familiar, shows the scale of festivals across the region.
✗ No temporal context — you land in a map with no obvious "where to start."
Best for: tourists, first-time users exploring geography.
★ Option B trade-offs:
✓ Opens to today's events — immediately useful during festival season.
✓ Date strip shows the whole week at a glance, multi-day spans visible.
✓ Users always know what's happening right now.
✗ Less geographic context on first open.
Best for: returning users, villagers, family planning visits.
Option C trade-offs:
✓ Chronological list is the most accessible for elderly users — no map interaction needed.
✓ Search is always visible.
✗ No geographic context. Harder to discover nearby festivals.
Best for: elderly users, comprehensive trip planning.
